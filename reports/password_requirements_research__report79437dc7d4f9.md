# Password Requirements Research

**Research identifier:** report-79437dc7d4f9
**Research objective:** What are the organization's password requirements?
**Status:** draft (final report)

## Findings

Based on searches of the indexed knowledge base, **no organization-specific password requirements were found**. The indexed sources do not contain password length, complexity, rotation, or expiration requirements for any organization.

Retrieved evidence:
- A search for "password requirements organization" returned no relevant information.
- A search for "password policy minimum length complexity" returned no relevant information.
- A search for "authentication credentials access control" returned general identity and access management (IAM) controls relevant to AI systems (role-based permissions, Least Privilege, Need-to-Know, periodic access reviews, rate limiting). These are IAM principles, not password-specific requirements.
- A search for "NCA password cyber security guidance" confirmed that the National Cybersecurity Authority's AI Cybersecurity Guidelines (AICG-1: 2026) do **not** include password-specific controls.

Inference:
- The indexed knowledge base covers AI governance, AI risk management, AI security, and related frameworks (NIST AI RMF, NIST GenAI Profile, OWASP LLM Top 10, ISO AI documents, SDAIA principles, NCA guidance, DGA guidance). None of these indexed sources, as retrieved, specify organizational password requirements.
- The only access-control-related evidence concerns AI-system IAM principles rather than end-user or administrator password rules.

Recommendation:
- If the objective is to document an organization's actual password requirements, the needed evidence is outside the currently indexed knowledge base. Obtain the organization's internal IT security policy, acceptable use policy, or applicable password standard (e.g., NIST SP 800-63B if relevant) from an authorized source.

## Evidence Limitations

- The indexed knowledge base does not contain password requirements for any specific organization.
- Searches returned only general AI-related IAM controls and confirmation that NCA AI guidance excludes password-specific rules.
- No document in the indexed sources was found that defines password length, complexity, history, or rotation requirements.
- This response is limited to the major indexed source families (NIST AI RMF, NIST GenAI Profile, OWASP LLM Top 10, ISO AI governance/security, SDAIA AI Ethics Principles, NCA guidance, DGA guidance, and other indexed AI governance/risk/security documents).

## Confidence

**Low** for answering the stated objective (organization's password requirements), because the required evidence is absent from the indexed knowledge base. **High** for the negative finding that no such password requirements appear in the retrieved indexed sources.

## Next Action

Obtain the organization's internal password/IT security policy or an applicable external password standard from an authorized source to answer the objective. No further indexed-source research can resolve this question with current evidence.
